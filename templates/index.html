About

sInterface is an opinionated replacement for the standard BlizzardUI. Reductionism is the goal. Fewer, smaller elements; frames that show when required; key information in the spotlight.

There is no in-game configuration, customising your installation requires modifying lua files. sInterface does not use character profilesâ€”configuration is shared between all characters without setup.

Installation

Standard

  • Locate your WoW install, likely C:/Program Files/World of Warcraft/_retail_
  • Unzip sInterface.zip
  • Move the extracted directory, sInterface/, into Interface/AddOns/
